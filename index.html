Recent Research

  • UAV Anomaly Detection and Recover via Tracking Structured Sparse Subspace

    UAV monitors the surrounding environment by equipped with flight key sensors, and it is easy for UAV to get abnormal sensors due to interference or other unexpected conditions. Therefore, it is of great significance to study the real-time online lightweight abnormal data detection and recovery method for UAV flight.

    In this paper, we focus on Real-time Anomaly Detection and recovery of multidimensional heterogeneous flight data. In view of the limitations of existing literature work and the challenges in the field of UAV flight data anomaly detection and recovery, this paper proposes an online UAV data anomaly detection and recovery scheme based on structured sparse subspace and dynamic threshold , which is a lightweight, unsupervised, model free real-time anomaly detection algorithm. The scheme consists of five parts: ① data receiving and processing, ② anomaly threshold dynamic adjustment, ③ weight and residual estimation, ④ online structured sparse subspace learning and ⑤ abnormal data recovery.

  • Data Tracking System based on Block-DAG and Spatiotemporal Blockchain

    This work uses blockchain block DAG architecture combined with BFV batch homomorphic encryption scheme to achieve spatiotemporal data retrieval and tracking. As a spatiotemporal data tracking system containing two dimensions of time and space, this work can be mainly applied to the fields of user behavior tracking and logistics supply.

    ① Support the privacy protection of whereabouts data. In this work, the user generated spatio-temporal positioning data is encrypted by AES, and the generated bloom filter is encrypted by BFV homomorphism to ensure that the data is transmitted in the form of ciphertext on the public channel.

    ② Efficient track data update and query. TPS 5000+. Compared with the traditional blockchain single chain architecture, block DAG implements parallel and asynchronous transactions, which greatly shortens the transaction encapsulation time and further speeds up the block building time. For a large number of queries on spatiotemporal data,this work adopts batch parallel BFV homomorphic encryption scheme, which greatly reduces the time consumed by data query.
